Veg Pulao Recipe

Description:

A classic vegetable pulao is a flavorful and aromatic one-pot Indian rice dish made with basmati rice, mixed vegetables, herbs, and mild spices. It is a healthy, comforting, and quick meal that is perfect for a weeknight dinner or for special occasions. The perfectly cooked rice grains should be fluffy, separate, and fragrant, not mushy or sticky

Ingredients

For the Pulo:

  • 1 ½ cups long-grain basmati rice, rinsed and soaked
  • 3 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1–2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • ¾ cup chopped mixed vegetables (carrots, green beans, peas, cauliflower)
  • 2 ½ cups water
  • Salt, to taste
  • ½ tsp garam masala powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the rice
  2. Sauté the spices and aromatics
  3. Cook the vegetables
  4. Combine and cook
  5. Finish the pulao
  6. Fluff and serve
