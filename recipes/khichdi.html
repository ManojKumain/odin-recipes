Khichdi Recipe

Description

Khichdi is a healthy, comforting, and wholesome one-pot Indian meal made from rice and lentils. It is often recommended during illness or for babies and the elderly because it is nutritious and easy to digest. While the simplest version includes just rice, lentils, salt, and turmeric, this recipe adds a tempering of spices, onions, and tomatoes for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup short-grain rice
  • ½ cup moong dal (split yellow lentils), rinsed
  • 3–4 cups water (adjust for desired consistency)
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp salt, or to taste
  • 1 tsp ghee

Instructions

  1. Prepare the rice and dal
  2. Cook the khichdi
  3. Pressure cook
  4. Prepare the tempering
  5. Finish and serve
