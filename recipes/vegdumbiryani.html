Veg Dum Biryani

Description

Veg dum biryani is a flavorful and aromatic one-pot vegetarian dish that is made by layering partially cooked basmati rice with marinated vegetables and spices. The pot is then sealed and cooked on a low flame (the "dum" method), allowing the flavors to meld together perfectly through the slow steaming process. This recipe is a vegetarian take on the classic Hyderabadi biryani and is a festive dish that is rich, royal, and delicious

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder (or to taste)
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tsp biryani masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves (cilantro)
  • 1 tsp salt, or to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables, cubed (e.g., carrots, potatoes, cauliflower, peas, beans)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the vegetables
  2. Fry the onions
  3. Cook the rice
  4. Make the biryani gravy
  5. Layer and seal for dum
  6. Cook on dum
  7. Serve
